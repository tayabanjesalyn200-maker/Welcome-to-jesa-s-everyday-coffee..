Photo Description & Analysis

1. What story does your photo tell?
My photos capture simple yet meaningful moments of everyday life, especially showing students going about their daily routines. It tells a story of movement, friendship, and the real, lively atmosphere of our community and school surroundings.
2. Why did you choose this subject?
I chose this subject because it captures real life and raw, unscripted moments. Every scene shows the beauty of normal days, the energy of people moving together, and the unique character of our neighborhood, which I find very interesting and relatable.
3. What elements such as lighting, composition, timing, & emotions make your photo effective?
The natural light makes the scenes bright and clear, showing the real colors and details of the environment. I composed the shots to balance the people and the background nicely, and waited for the perfect timing to capture natural actions. The photos also show calm and lively emotions that make viewers feel the authentic vibe of the place.
4. What challenges did you encounter while taking the photo?
It was challenging to find the right angle because there were many things happening around at the same time, like moving vehicles and people. I also had to wait patiently for the perfect moment and adjust to different lighting conditions to get a clear and nice shot.
5. How can you improve your shot next time?
Next time, I will explore more unique and creative angles, practice faster shooting to catch sudden moments, and adjust camera settings properly to get better lighting, focus, and overall balance.
